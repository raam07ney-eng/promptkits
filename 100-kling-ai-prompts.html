Frequently Asked Questions

Do these prompts work with earlier versions of Kling AI?
Yes. While optimised for Kling AI 3.0, these prompts also work with earlier Kling AI versions. The cinematic language is compatible with all versions of the model.
What is the difference between Kling AI cinematic mode and standard mode?
Cinematic mode in Kling AI 3.0 produces higher quality lighting, more film-like grain and colour grading, and more deliberate camera movement. It is recommended for all creative and storytelling content.
Can I use these prompts with other AI video tools?
Yes. The prompt structure works with Runway, Sora, Pika, and LumaLabs as well. The Kling AI specific mode references at the end of each prompt can simply be ignored by other tools.
How long should Kling AI 3.0 clips be for best quality?
Kling AI 3.0 produces the best quality results at 5-10 seconds. Longer clips can lose coherence and quality. For sequences requiring longer duration, generate multiple shorter clips and edit them together.
