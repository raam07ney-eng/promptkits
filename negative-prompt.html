Frequently asked questions

What is a negative prompt?

A negative prompt tells the AI what NOT to include in the image — helping eliminate bad anatomy, extra fingers, blurry faces, artifacts, and low quality results.

Where do I paste the negative prompt?

In Stable Diffusion and SDXL, there is a dedicated Negative Prompt input field below the main prompt box. In Midjourney, add it after your main prompt with the --no parameter.

Will this fix bad hands and fingers?

Yes — select Hands focus and the tool generates specific negative prompts targeting extra fingers, fused hands, and distorted hand anatomy.

Is this free?

Yes, completely free. No account or sign-up needed.
