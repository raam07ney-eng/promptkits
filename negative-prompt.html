Frequently asked questions

What is a negative prompt?

A negative prompt tells the AI what NOT to include in the image. It helps eliminate common problems like bad anatomy, extra fingers, blurry faces, artifacts, and low quality results.

Where do I paste the negative prompt?

In Stable Diffusion and SDXL, there is a dedicated "Negative Prompt" input field below the main prompt box. In Midjourney, add it after your main prompt with the --no parameter.

Will this fix bad hands and fingers?

Yes — select "Hands" focus and the tool generates specific negative prompts targeting extra fingers, fused hands, and distorted hand anatomy which are common issues in AI image generation.
