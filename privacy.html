Last updated: June 2024

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our tools. However, we may collect the following information automatically:

When you use our prompt tools, the text you enter is sent to our server to generate an enhanced prompt. We do not store, log, or share the prompts you enter. Each request is processed and discarded immediately.
