What makes a prompt cinematic?

A cinematic prompt does more than describe what is in the scene. It describes how the scene is captured — and this distinction is everything. When you tell an AI image or video tool "a woman standing in a forest", you are describing the subject. When you tell it "a close-up of a woman's face in dappled forest light, 85mm anamorphic lens, golden hour backlight creating rim separation, shallow depth of field, teal and orange colour grade, film grain", you are describing the cinematography.

Shot type
Wide, medium, close-up, extreme close-up, bird's eye, low angle
Lens choice
24mm wide, 35mm standard, 85mm portrait, anamorphic
Lighting
Golden hour, Rembrandt, split, soft window, neon practical
Colour grade
Teal and orange, cold desaturated, warm vintage, natural
Camera movement
Dolly push-in, tracking, crane rising, orbital, handheld
Atmosphere
Morning mist, rain, dust in light, smoke, golden hour glow
