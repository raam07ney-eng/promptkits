Frequently asked questions

What is an AI video prompt generator?

It takes a simple scene description and expands it into a detailed prompt with camera movements, lighting, pacing, and style details that produce much better results in video AI tools like Kling AI, Runway, and Sora.

What are Cinematic Controls?

Cinematic controls let you set professional filmmaking parameters — lens type, color grade, motion speed, lighting style — giving you director-level control over the AI video output.

Which video AI tools work best with these prompts?

Kling AI and Runway Gen-3 respond best to detailed prompts. Sora and Pika also support rich descriptions. Always match the tool selection to the platform you're using.
