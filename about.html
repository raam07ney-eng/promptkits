A VFX compositor with a decade in professional production

PromptKits is built and maintained by a VFX compositor with 12 years of industry experience. The work spans feature films, visual effects pipelines, and digital content production — with deep hands-on expertise in compositing, paint and prep work, wire and rig removal, and clean plate generation.

What PromptKits is

Professional cinematography language, made accessible

PromptKits is a free collection of AI prompt tools and a curated library of over 1150 ready-to-use prompts — written with real filmmaking principles. Every prompt specifies the camera movement, lens type, lighting setup, colour grade, and mood using the exact vocabulary that AI video models respond to best.

The library covers video prompts (cinematic, horror, drone shots, sci-fi, nature, action sports, time-lapse, character study, Kling AI) and image prompts (Midjourney portraits, food photography, wedding cinematics, portrait lighting) — all written from a VFX professional's understanding of what makes a shot work.
