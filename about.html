About PromptKits

Free AI prompt tools
built for everyone

PromptKits is a free collection of AI prompt generation tools designed to help creators, artists, filmmakers, writers, and everyday users get better results from AI tools like Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Kling AI, Runway, ChatGPT, and Claude.

Our mission

AI tools are powerful — but getting good results depends entirely on how well you can describe what you want. Most people struggle with writing effective prompts, which leads to frustrating, mediocre outputs.

PromptKits bridges that gap. Our tools take your basic ideas and transform them into detailed, optimized prompts — giving everyone access to professional-quality AI results, regardless of experience level.

Our tools

🖼️
Image Prompt Generator
Generate detailed image prompts for Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly, and Leonardo AI — with professional controls for camera angle, lens, lighting, and more.
🎬
Video Prompt Generator
Create cinematic video prompts for Kling AI, Runway, Sora, and Pika with full control over camera movement, color grade, motion speed, and lens type.
🔍
Image to Prompt
Upload any image and get a detailed AI prompt to recreate or get inspired by it. Perfect for reverse-engineering AI art styles.
🚫
Negative Prompt Generator
Generate effective negative prompts to eliminate bad anatomy, artifacts, and unwanted elements from Stable Diffusion and SDXL images.
✨
Prompt Enhancer
Transform any basic prompt into a detailed, optimized version for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney, and more.
